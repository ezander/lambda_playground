#*
  Tutorial
  ========

  This is a short introduction into the untyped λ calculus focussing on the
  computational aspects of it, and not so much on how to do proofs. But maybe

  Note: the best way to view the tutorial is to maximize the window and enable
  theater mode. When you click on any tutorial or example link, it will be
  loaded into the scratch buffer. There you can modify it and play around with
  it however you want. You undo can undo things, and after undoing all your
  changes, it will also bring you back to the last loaded buffer. If you want
  you can also save your experiments into your own buffers (see [doc/Buffers] ).
  If you want to try this example/undo thing just click here:
  [example/Bool Implementations] and then click 'undo' to come back here.


  Note: this tutorial is currently far from finished: to get a better
  introduction into the lambda calculus at the moment, please look into the
  literature ([doc/Literature]) or take a look at the examples.

  The only currently working section of the tutorial is this one here:
  [tutorial/Church booleans]
