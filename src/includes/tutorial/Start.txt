#*
  Tutorial
  ========

  This is a short introduction into the untyped λ calculus focussing on the
  computational aspects and not so much on how to do deep proofs with it.

  Note: the best way to view the tutorial is to go maximize the window and enable theater mode
  loaded into scratch buffer, can modify, undo
  undo goes back, after all changes undone
  
  A little bit of the history and the whys and hows of LC can be found in the next
  part of this series [tutorial/Intro].
  
  Or if you want to jump straight into it skipping the historical background go
  here [tutorial/Church booleans]
  
  Some literature on LC can also be found here: [tutorial/Literature]
