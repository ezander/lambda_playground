#* 
  Some notes on the language
  ==========================
 
*#

# Outer parenthesis may be omitted
≡ (λx . (M)) (λx . M)

# Application is left-associative
≡ ((M N) L) (M N L)
≢ (M (N L)) (M N L)

# Abstraction is right associative
≡ (λx . (λy . M)) (λx . λy . M)

# Application takes precedence over abstraction
≡ (λx . (M N)) (λx . M N)
≢ ((λx . M) N) (λx . M N)
